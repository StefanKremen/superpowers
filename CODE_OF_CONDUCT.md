# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Members, contributors, leaders pledge harassment-free community participation for all, regardless of age, body size, visible/invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity/expression, experience level, education, socio-economic status, nationality, appearance, race, religion, sexual identity/orientation.

Pledge: act/interact toward open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, healthy community.

## Our Standards

Positive behavior examples:

* Empathy + kindness toward others
* Respect differing opinions, viewpoints, experiences
* Give + accept constructive feedback gracefully
* Accept responsibility, apologize for mistakes, learn from it
* Focus on community good, not just self

Unacceptable behavior examples:

* Sexualized language/imagery, sexual attention/advances
* Trolling, insults, derogatory comments, personal/political attacks
* Public/private harassment
* Publishing others' private info (physical/email address) without permission
* Other conduct inappropriate in professional setting

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Leaders clarify + enforce standards. Take fair corrective action against inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful behavior.

Leaders may remove, edit, reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, contributions misaligned with this Code. Communicate moderation reasons when appropriate.

## Scope

Applies in all community spaces + when individual officially represents community in public. Examples: official email, official social media, appointed representative at online/offline event.

## Enforcement

Report abuse/harassment to leaders at jesse@primeradiant.com. All complaints reviewed + investigated promptly + fairly.

Leaders must respect reporter privacy + security.

## Enforcement Guidelines

Leaders follow these Community Impact Guidelines for violations:

### 1. Correction

**Community Impact**: Inappropriate language or unprofessional/unwelcome behavior.

**Consequence**: Private written warning from leaders. Clarity on violation + why behavior inappropriate. Public apology may be requested.

### 2. Warning

**Community Impact**: Violation via single incident or action series.

**Consequence**: Warning with consequences for continued behavior. No interaction with involved parties (including unsolicited contact with enforcers) for set period. Covers community spaces + external channels like social media. Violation → temporary or permanent ban.

### 3. Temporary Ban

**Community Impact**: Serious violation, including sustained inappropriate behavior.

**Consequence**: Temporary ban from all interaction/public communication with community for set period. No public/private contact with involved parties (including unsolicited contact with enforcers) during period. Violation → permanent ban.

### 4. Permanent Ban

**Community Impact**: Pattern of violation, sustained inappropriate behavior, harassment of individual, or aggression/disparagement toward classes of individuals.

**Consequence**: Permanent ban from all public interaction within community.
